About Me

I'm a front-end web developer from Tunisia, Sfax. I'm currently still learning from various sources to improve my skills. I got my computer science degree in the year of 2018 at the Institute of computer science and multimedia of Sfax. During my time at ISIMS, I was a member of the Microsoft tech-club for 2 years. Most of my development knowledge came from FreeCodeCamp.

I have a keen eye for design and you might see that in my projects. I try my best to make the things I build look beautiful. I like playing video games, watching TV-shows, listening to podcasts and I enjoy the company of like-minded people.
